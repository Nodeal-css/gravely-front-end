Gravely
Digital Cemetery

Gravely is a software-as-a-service platform that aims to facilitate and improve the management and monitoring of cemeteries. It addresses the problems that cemetery visitors and owners encounter, such as difficulty locating graves, lack of information about the deceased, challenges organizing records, and complexity in planning burials. It offers a solution that enhancing the visitor experience, preserving the historical and cultural heritage, streamlining the administrative processes, and optimizing land use.
